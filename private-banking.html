THE BEST WAY FORWARD

REALISE THE TRUE VALUE OF YOUR WEALTH

 

At Lloyds Bank Private Banking we understand that many of our customers lead demanding lives and have needs that go beyond conventional banking. Thats why we take care of them personally, introducing them to specialist partners to ensure they safely navigate the complex world of wealth.

We call it relationship-led banking.'

Relationship-led banking services

At the heart of our service is a personal banking experience that provides access to specialist Wealth Services. However our stand-alone services are available to eligible customers without having to open a Private Banking account.

Private Banking

Our Private Banking service provides you with a dedicated named contact who supports your day to day banking needs, ensures you benefit from exclusive products and introduces you to specialist partners when required.

More about our banking service

Stand-alone services

The following services are available to customers even if they are not eligible for our Private Banking service:

Specialist Lending

If you have a complex or unique borrowing requirement our Specialist Lending team are on hand to help you find the best way to finance your ambitions.

  • Specialist Lending Find out more

Estate Administration

Our Estate Administration Service can relieve the burden placed on Executors and next of kin by taking care of all the financial, legal, tax and personal matters that arise following bereavement.

  • Estate Administration Find out more

Professional Trustees

Our Professional Trustee Services through Lloyds Bank can help you preserve assets for future generations by setting up a trust in your lifetime or transfer an existing trust. 

  • Trustees Services Find out more

Financial Advice

Our partnership with Schroders Personal Wealth gives you access to expert Schroders Personal Wealth Advisors who are committed to bringing your financial plans to life. Eligibility Criteria, Fees and Charges Apply.

  • More about Financial Advice Find out more

Service eligibility

Lloyds Bank Private Banking offers two levels of service:

Our Private Banking service is available to customers with 250,000 or more in savings and/or investments or a mortgage of 750,000 or more with Lloyds Bank.

And our Mayfair Banking service is available to clients with 2 million or more in savings and/or investments or an annual income of 500,000 or more.
